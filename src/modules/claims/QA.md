## Manual QA checklist

- Summary tab: change time filter, view KPI cards render; click “View all” in Needs Attention opens tasks tab and selecting a task opens detail panel.
- Workflow tab: toggle between Kanban/Table; select/deselect rows; expand AI suggestions and rejection details; open a claim to show detail panel.
- Tasks tab: bulk select toggles header controls; assignment dropdown opens/closes; row click opens claim detail panel.
- Detail panel: switch between all tabs; expand/collapse panel width; toggle CMS-1500 visibility; select/deselect AI suggestions; ensure timeline entries show for submitted/accepted/rejected claims.
